FAQ

Does AIRE replace a full due-diligence process?
No. It speeds screening and standardizes underwriting; always verify details.

Where does data come from?
From manual inputs and configured providers. Reports include provenance (source + confidence).

Can my team share templates?
Yes. Templates are scoped to workspaces for team consistency.

Is there an API?
Yes. Create API keys in the app, then call /v1/grade from the backend service.
