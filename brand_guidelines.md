# AIRE Terminal — Brand Guidelines (Starter)

## Positioning
**Paste link → investment-grade decision.** AIRE is a real estate underwriting terminal for investors and teams.

## Voice
- Confident, technical, concise
- Avoid hype; emphasize standardization and speed
- Always include: **Not financial advice**

## Colors
- Background: #0B1220
- Panels: #0F1A2F / #111F38
- Brand: #5B8CFF (primary), #7CF7C4 (accent)
- Text: #EAF1FF
- Muted: #A9B8D6

## Typography
- UI: system-ui
- Code/terminal: ui-monospace

## Logo usage
- Use on dark background with padding
- Do not stretch; keep square icon + wordmark lockup
